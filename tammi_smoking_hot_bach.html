Anakeesta

Mountain-top playground above downtown Gatlinburg. Plan 3-4 hours up top.

Things to do up there
  • Chondola or Ridge Rambler — scenic ride up the mountain
  • Treetop Skywalk — 880 ft of hanging bridges, 50-60 ft up in the trees
  • AnaVista Tower — 360° views of the Smokies
  • Hellbender Mountain Coaster — double-rail, 3,350 ft, banked turns
  • Rail Runner Mountain Coaster — single-rail, 25 mph, only one in the US
  • Vista Gardens — botanical walking paths with sculptures
  • TreeVenture Challenge — netted treehouses & rope bridges
  • Firefly Village — mountaintop shopping & dining
  • Dueling Ziplines — extra cost (~$60), race a friend down

Wild Plum Tea Room 🌿
★ 4.3 · ~$25-40/pp · Vegan/vegetarian focused

Charming spot in the Arts & Crafts Community. Vegan and vegetarian-friendly menu, made-from-scratch everything. Salmon burger, lobster pie, beet salad, famous Wild Plum muffins. Reservations a must. Note: open Thursday-Saturday only, lunch hours till 3 PM — best as a Saturday lunch instead.

★ 4.3 · ~$25-35/pp · Local & affordable

Small, cozy, beloved local spot focused on fresh Smoky Mountain trout — grilled, blackened, parmesan, smoked. Reasonable prices (~$20/head), homemade hushpuppies. No reservations, so come early or put name in.

🎢 Pigeon Forge options
Dollywood
★ 4.7 · ~$94/person · ~22 mi · ~35 min

World-class theme park with coasters, shows, food, and Dolly Parton everything. Full-day commitment — plan 6-8 hours. Lightning Rod & Big Bear Mountain are bucket-list coasters. Best value if you spend the whole day.

Navigate Tickets
The Island in Pigeon Forge
★ 4.5 · Free entry · pay per ride · ~22 mi · ~35 min

Open-air entertainment complex. Free to walk around — pay only for what you do. The 200-ft Great Smoky Mountain Wheel (~$18) is the headliner. Tons of shops, fudge, mini bowling, ropes course, dancing fountains, and food. Easy half-day, low commitment.

Navigate
TopJump Trampoline & Extreme Arena
★ 4.6 · ~$28/hr/person · ~22 mi

Huge indoor trampoline park — wall-to-wall trampolines, foam pits, ninja warrior course, dodgeball, candy store. High-energy fun for an hour or two. Wear socks (or buy grip socks there).

Navigate Call
WonderWorks
★ 4.4 · ~$36/person · ~22 mi

The upside-down building you can't miss on the Parkway. Interactive science museum with 100+ hands-on exhibits, ropes course, laser tag, 4D theater. Great rainy-day backup. ~2-3 hours inside.

Navigate Call
Titanic Museum
★ 4.7 · ~$34/person · ~22 mi

Two-story ship-shaped museum with 450+ real Titanic artifacts. You get a boarding pass for an actual passenger and find out at the end if they survived. Takes ~2 hours, totally immersive.

Navigate Call
Pigeon Forge Mountain Coasters
★ 4.6 · ~$20-25/ride · ~22 mi

Multiple coasters on the Parkway — Smoky Mountain Alpine Coaster (the original, longest in US), Rocky Top Mountain Coaster, Goats on the Roof, Ole Smoky Mountain Coaster. Pick whichever's least busy.
